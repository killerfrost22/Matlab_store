Consider the two approximate solutions x1 = [−0.0827 0.5]^T, and x2 = [0.999 − 1.001]^T Using the norm command, compute (a) the relative forward errors for x1 and x2 using the 2-norm (b) the relative backward errors for x1 and x2 Comment on the size of your backward and forward errors.
